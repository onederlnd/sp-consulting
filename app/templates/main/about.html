About the Firm

We Work
Differently.

Sunceray Patterson Consulting has operated for over a decade at the intersection of business strategy and enterprise technology. We're not a big four firm. We're not a staffing agency. We're a focused team of practitioners who get involved, stay accountable, and don't leave until the work is done.

Honest Counsel

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Every engagement starts with a clear-eyed assessment — even when that's uncomfortable.

Embedded Partnership

We work alongside your team, not above it. Our advisors operate inside the problem, not at arm's length from a slide deck.

Measurable Outcomes

Every engagement has defined success metrics set at the start. We don't declare victory — results do.

Long-Term Orientation

We build relationships, not transactions. Most of our clients have worked with us across multiple engagements and years.
