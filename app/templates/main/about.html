About the Firm

We Work
Differently.

Sunceray Patterson Consulting has operated for over a decade at the intersection of business strategy and enterprise technology. We're not a big four firm. We're not a staffing agency. We're a focused team of practitioners who get involved, stay accountable, and don't leave until the work is done.

Honest Counsel

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Every engagement starts with a clear-eyed assessment — even when that's uncomfortable.

Embedded Partnership

We work alongside your team, not above it. Our advisors operate inside the problem, not at arm's length from a slide deck.

Measurable Outcomes

Every engagement has defined success metrics set at the start. We don't declare victory — results do.

Long-Term Orientation

We build relationships, not transactions. Most of our clients have worked with us across multiple engagements and years.

The Team

Sunceray Patterson

Founder - Principal Consultant

Sunceray Patterson is a founder and technology strategist based in Detroit, Michigan, working at the intersection of strategy, emerging technology, and execution. Her approach is rooted in systems thinking and applied technical expertise, focused on translating complex ideas into structured, scalable, operational solutions.

She holds a Bachelor of Science in Public Policy, Planning, and Management from the University of Oregon, and strengthened her technical foundation through a coding bootcamp before founding Immirtual in 2022 — a venture focused on immersive and emerging technologies. Through this work, she participated in selective accelerator programs including Techstars Founder Catalyst, FIU Venture Miami, and the Alan B. Levan | NSU Broward Center of Innovation.

Currently, she is building within the consulting and technology space, with an emphasis on artificial intelligence, immersive systems, and applied innovation — helping organizations move emerging technologies from concept to execution in ways that are practical, scalable, and impactful.
